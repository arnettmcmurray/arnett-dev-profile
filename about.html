Who is Arnett?

I'm the kind of guy who will fix your code, season your cast iron, and still find time to walk the dog before sunrise. A former chef who worked in Michelin-starred kitchens, I now write software with the same precision I once plated foie gras — clean, intentional, and bold.

I grew up on the North Side of Chicago, raised in the city’s rhythm and grit. I’ve run soccer fields, coached youth sports, and rebuilt my backyard with nothing but a few chains, some lumber, and stubbornness. These days, I’m more likely to be debugging JavaScript or fine-tuning a SQL query, but I bring that same discipline and hustle to every project I touch.

I don’t just study systems — I build them. Whether it's a terminal HUD, a portfolio site, or a full-stack application, I want things to work well and look sharp. I'm obsessed with the process and always curious about the "why" behind the "what."

I’ve helped others navigate complicated life situations and taught myself how to untangle problems most people run from. That perspective fuels my work — thoughtful, resilient, and unafraid to go deep where others quit.

Outside the screen glow, you’ll find me grilling in the yard, working on DIY builds, or taking inspiration from the chaos that my pets bring into my workspace. I’m building a career that blends creativity, logic, and integrity — one clean line of code at a time.
